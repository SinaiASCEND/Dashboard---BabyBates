Framework & sources cited within the text

The Core + Cluster physical examination framework harmonizes two strands of scholarship referenced in the source material:

  • The national consensus “core” maneuvers described by Gowda and colleagues, Academic Medicine (2014).
  • The early-learner cluster-based instructional model validated by Jewett and colleagues, BMC Medical Education (2024).
  • Harmonized through faculty review at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai.

FULL and PARTIAL performance descriptors are offered as examples of clear performance criteria that programs may adapt to their own learners and assessment systems.
